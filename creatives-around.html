This project was done in a team as part of the final project at DCI.

Creatives Around is a location-based social platform for visual artists. The idea of the app is to connect with other visual artists in close proximity and discover the local art community. The app suggests profiles based on location and art forms. Once the connection is confirmed by both sides, direct messaging between the users is available and the users are added as a connection.

The mission of the app is to hold space for visual artists, from various fields and levels of skill, to connect - inspire one another - and collaborate.
