Creatives Around

This was a project I did during my studies at DCI as the final project. Unfortunatelly the backend developer dropped off due to dutied he had at work. so the backend is yet to be completed.

Here is a short description of the idea: Creatives Around location-based social platform for visual artists. The idea of the app is to connect with other visual artists that are in your area. The app suggests profiles based on location and art forms. Once the connection is confirmed by both sides, direct messaging between the users is available and the users are added as a connection.

The mission of the app is to hold space for visual artists, from various fields and levels of skill, to connect - inspire one another - and collaborate.
